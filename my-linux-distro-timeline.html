  3.  Manjaro
    • November 2023 - October 2024

  4.  EndeavourOS
    • October 2024 - until my main machine died ⚰️
    • Nothing grand happened - just vibes
    • pacman -Sybau
    • Finally started using i3 and pywal , real man ricing
    • Now left behind on my dead computer's corpse - alone, cold, still seeking startup 🥀
  5.  Debian
    • After main machine's death - about 6 months of use
    • Found an ancient netbook and tried Artix, Xubuntu, and Debian
    • Artix and Debian both worked, but I went with Debian for a new taste
    • Stable, until the first update screwed a GRUB config. Thankfully, it's a documented issue
    • Fastfetch isn't just named `fastfetch` - it's fast, even on slow.
    • Rock-solid overall - even ran smoothly on an Intel Atom N2600 in 2024 💪
  6.  Void Linux
    • July 2025 - present
    • Got a new computer, so I entered the Void 🪐
    • runit instead of systemd - fast, minimal and easier to use
    • Solid, solid, solid… but some packages doesn't exist in XBPS 🥲
